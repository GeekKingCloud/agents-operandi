# Research Signal Rubric

Use this rubric when turning messy sources into a practical recommendation.

## Verdicts

- **Actually useful:** primary evidence or reproducible demo supports a change worth trying.
- **Maybe useful:** plausible and relevant, but situational or not yet proven.
- **Mostly vibes:** interesting framing but little evidence.
- **Unsupported/wrong:** contradicted by primary sources or missing necessary proof.
- **Needs testing:** could matter, but only a local experiment will answer it.

## Evidence quality

Prefer direct docs, code, releases, issues, papers, transcripts, and measured behavior. Treat screenshots, unsourced threads, and engagement metrics as weak evidence.

## Output bar

The user should know what to do next, what to ignore, and what uncertainty remains.
